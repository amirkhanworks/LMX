About Luminox

Built by Engineers,
For the Real World.

Luminox Automation started with a simple idea: smart home technology should work for every home, not just new ones. That idea became a patent-pending product and then a full IoT engineering capability for clients globally.

Fahad Khan
Founder & CEO
Dec 2019 – Present · Mumbai

Fahad is an electrical engineer specialising in IoT systems, and the founder of Luminox Automation.

He started Luminox in 2019 to solve a problem in his own home and built a retrofit smart home system that is now patent-pending in India. Since then, he has expanded into B2B IoT engineering, delivering projects for clients in India and Qatar.

His hands-on work spans custom PCB design, embedded firmware, wearable biometrics, cellular connectivity, and cloud integration.

Our Story

From One Home to Global Projects

2019
Luminox Automation founded
First retrofit smart home module built for Fahad's own home in Mumbai.
2020
ISB Pre-Accelerator Programme
Business model refined. First external customers onboarded.
2021
First batch of homes connected
Smart home product deployed across initial homes in Mumbai Metropolitan Region.
2022
First B2B IoT service project delivered
Industrial sensor deployment. Services vertical established.
2023
CamelX project initiated
First GCC market engagement. Custom wearable biometric tracker for SurgIQ, Qatar.
2024
CamelX deployed in Qatar
Patent application filed in India. System live at race-day events.
2025
